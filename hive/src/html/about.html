About BOP

BOP (Bisexual Organizing Project) is a 501©(3) non-profit registered in Minnesota that serves the Upper Midwest. We are committed to building bi+ communities -- including those who identify as bisexual, pansexual, fluid, queer, and unlabeled -- through regular events and the yearly national BECAUSE conference. BOP is welcoming and inclusive of everyone, including but not limited to people of all gender identities, sexual orientations, sexes, relationship orientations, ethnicities, abilities, religions, and political affiliations.

BOP also works in partnership with other groups and individuals in the Upper Midwest who are organizing events serving bisexual and other nonmonosexual communities.

Our Mission

We seek to build, serve and advocate for an empowered bi+ community, including those who identify as bisexual, pansexual, fluid, queer, and/or unlabeled.

Our Vision

Within the next five years grow Bisexual Organizing Project (BOP) annual funding to $100,000 and increase our community building, education, and advocacy for bi+ communities, including those who identify as bisexual, pansexual, fluid, queer, and/or unlabeled.

Our History

Bisexual Organizing Project (BOP) grew out of community organizations operating in the Twin Cities in the 1980s and 1990s. BOP was founded by Minnesota bisexual leaders who were active in local, regional, and national bi+ organizing. In 1999, BOP incorporated as a Minnesota nonprofit and earned IRS 501©(3) status.
